What kind of town would you find fun?
\nWhat is the shape of the roads? The shape of the building? What kind of functions are necessary?<\/p>\n

Without being stuck in the real world, children freely imagine their ideal town\/city, and will use large materials such as cardboard, clay, paper and cloth to make big models that express their ideal city.
\nThe target site for consideration is the city’s in the Kaihin Makuhari area.
\nWhat kind of city would you like there to be in the future of Kaihin Makuhari?
\nThis is not only an opportunity for children to think about their own lives from a higher perspective, but the ideas of the children may give new feelings and ideas to adults.<\/p>\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n
