1996 \u2013 2000, he worked in Tadao Ando Architect & Associates. In 2002, Master of Science in Advanced Architectural Design from Columbia University Graduate School of Architecture, Planning and Preservation (AAD).<\/p>\n

2002 \u2013 2006, he subsequently worked for SHoP Architects (New York). Based in Tokyo and Taipei since 2007, and set up Noiz architects with Jia-Shuan Tsai. (Kosuke Sakai also became a partner since 2016).<\/p>\n

He does designs, installations, consulting, etc. applying digital technology centering on architecture both in Japan and abroad.
\nSince 2017, co-presided with Mr. Mitsuhiro Kaneda and Mr. Tetsuji Kuroda, a technology-based consulting platform \u201cgluon\u201d in architecture and urban context, as well as adjunct professor at the Art Media Center of Tokyo University of the Arts, Art Media Center, and at SFC Environmental Information Faculty of Keio University, Assistant Professor at Institute of Architecture of National Chiao Tung University in Taiwan.
