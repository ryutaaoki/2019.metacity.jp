Born in 1985 in Tokyo.
\nAssistant Curator at 21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art, Kanazawa
\nGraduated from the graduated school of Fine Arts<\/p>\n

His most major projects at the 21st Century Museum of Contemporary Art in Kanazawa include “DeathLAB: Democratizing Death” (2018-2019), “Undying Life” (2017-2018), “Ghost in the Cell”(2015-2016) etc. Other recent exhibitions “Frankenstein 2018″ (*Japanese Title) (GYRE, 2018) etc. His main lectures include ” Renaissance by Transhumanism ” (*Japanese Title) (Tokyo University, 2015) and etc. Major issues include “DeathLAB: Democratize death” (URBAN Magazine “Mortem” , the students of Urban Planning at Columbia University\u2019s Graduate School of Architecture, 2018), ” Semi-Living Material Issues on Bio-Fashion ” (*Japanese Title) “Vanitas 005” (Adachi Press, 2018) and etc. Art journal (bijutsutecho) issued in January 2018 “Special feature bio art” editing cooperation.
