When Souryo Matsumura was wandering around Europe, he became keenly aware of his ignorance about Japanese culture. As soon as he returned home, he launched himself into the world of Japanese tea ceremonies.
\nHe started the Shuhally project with the purpose of making tea ceremony more accessible and enjoyable.
\nWhile keeping the basics of tea ceremony, he has created his own style that adds ingenuity that matches the modern era.
\nHe has had invitations from ten different countries so far including the prime minister of Japan and his cabinet and produced many tea ceremonies.
\nWe are actively collaborating with other genres such as contemporary art, dance, human beat boxes, manga and etc.
